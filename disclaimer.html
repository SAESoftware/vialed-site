Medical Disclaimer

Vialed is a tracking and reminder tool for injectable medications. It is not a medical device.

Intended Use

Vialed is intended for use by adults (18 years or older) to track injectable medications that have been prescribed and approved by a qualified healthcare professional. Vialed is for human use only and is not intended for veterinary medications or use with pets or animals.

If you are under 18, pregnant, nursing, planning to become pregnant, have a chronic medical condition, or take multiple medications, consult your healthcare provider before using Vialed or any tracking tool to manage your medications.

What Vialed Does

Vialed helps you organize information about medications you already use, calculate volumes based on numbers you provide, log your doses, and receive reminders. All calculations are based on the information you enter.

What Vialed Does Not Do

Vialed does not:

  • Provide medical advice, diagnose any condition, or prescribe any treatment
  • Replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional
  • Check for drug interactions, allergies, or contraindications
  • Verify the appropriateness of any medication or dose for your specific situation
  • Account for individual variations in metabolism, body composition, or medical history
  • Substitute for reading the actual labeling and prescribing information for your medications

Using Vialed does not establish a doctor-patient relationship with anyone associated with the app.

Your Responsibility

You are solely responsible for:

  • Verifying every dose, calculation, and injection technique with your healthcare provider before use
  • Confirming that medications you track are appropriate for your specific medical situation
  • Reading and following the actual labeling, prescribing information, and your healthcare provider's instructions
  • Checking for potential interactions with other medications, supplements, or conditions
  • Recognizing and responding appropriately to any adverse reactions
  • Maintaining your own records independent of the app

The calculations and reminders provided by Vialed are for reference only. You must independently confirm them against your prescription, your physician's instructions, and the actual labeling of your medications.

Information You Enter

Vialed performs calculations based only on what you input. If you enter incorrect information — including total mg in vial, concentration, dose, schedule, syringe type, or capacity — Vialed's outputs will be incorrect. Always double-check your entries against your medication's actual labeling and your provider's instructions.

Medication Safety

Never start, stop, or change any medication or dose based on information in Vialed. Always consult your prescribing healthcare provider for medical decisions.

If you experience adverse effects from any medication, stop use and contact your healthcare provider immediately.

If you experience a medical emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number. Do not rely on Vialed for emergency medical information.

If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm or are in a mental health crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) or your local emergency services.

App Availability

Vialed may not always be available. The app could be unavailable due to phone failure, software issues, lost data, or other technical problems. Do not rely on Vialed as your only source of information about your medications. Always maintain independent records and follow your healthcare provider's instructions even when the app is unavailable.

No Guarantee of Accuracy

While we strive for accuracy, Vialed makes no warranty that the information, calculations, or reminders are error-free, complete, current, or appropriate for your specific situation. Vialed's data and calculations may contain errors. Use of Vialed is at your own risk.

Jurisdictional Limitations

Vialed is intended for use in jurisdictions where it is legally permitted. You are responsible for ensuring that your use of Vialed and the medications you track comply with the laws of your jurisdiction. Vialed makes no representations regarding the legality or regulatory status of any medication in your location.

Limitation of Liability

By using Vialed, you acknowledge that you understand these limitations and accept responsibility for your own medical decisions. Vialed, its developers, and its affiliates are not liable for any injury, illness, adverse consequence, financial loss, or other harm resulting from your use of the app or reliance on its information.

Questions

If you have questions about your medications, doses, injection technique, or potential interactions, consult a qualified healthcare professional. Vialed cannot answer medical questions.
