Privacy Policy

Draft. This Privacy Policy is a working draft prepared by the Vialed team and has not yet been reviewed by counsel. It will be revised before public launch. Send questions to support@vialed.app.

Vialed is built so that you don't have to trust us with your data. Everything you enter is stored on your iPhone. We have no servers that store your medication, vial, or dose information. We don't collect analytics. We don't show ads. We don't sell anything to anyone.

Who We Are

"Vialed" is an iOS application developed and published by SAE Software LLC, a Minnesota limited liability company ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how the Vialed app handles your information.

Information Stored on Your Device

Vialed lets you record information about your medications, vials, doses, schedules, and injection sites. This information includes whatever you choose to enter, which may include:

  • Medication names, custom labels, categories, notes, and color coding
  • Vial details — strength, reconstitution volume, lot number, acquisition date, expiration, cost, and notes
  • Dose records — date and time administered, amount, injection site, and notes
  • Schedule preferences and reminder settings
  • App preferences and onboarding state

All of this information is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is not transmitted to SAE Software LLC and is not visible to us.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device and choose to use Vialed's iCloud sync feature, your Vialed data is synced through your private iCloud account to your other Apple devices. This sync goes directly between your devices and Apple's iCloud service. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's handling of your iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

You can disable Vialed's use of iCloud at any time in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

Information We Do Not Collect

Vialed does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, telemetry, or crash-reporting SDKs. We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics, screen views, taps, session duration, or feature usage
  • Crash logs (other than the anonymous, aggregated reports Apple may provide to developers if you have opted into sharing diagnostic data with Apple in iOS Settings — these are handled by Apple, not us)
  • Health data from Apple Health or any other source — Vialed does not request HealthKit permissions
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or other device sensors

Vialed does not require an account. There is no sign-up, no login, and no password.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Vialed schedules local notifications on your device to remind you of doses and schedule events. These notifications are delivered by iOS using Apple's UserNotifications framework and do not pass through any server we operate. We do not send push notifications from a server.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Vialed Premium is sold as an in-app subscription processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We do not see or receive your payment information — Apple handles the entire transaction. The information Apple shares with us is limited to whether your Apple ID has an active Vialed subscription (we receive a signed transaction object verifying entitlement). Apple's handling of your purchase information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and the App Store Terms.

Children's Privacy

Vialed is intended for adults 18 years of age or older, as described in the Medical Disclaimer. Vialed is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Data Export and Deletion

Because your data lives only on your device (and, if enabled, in your iCloud account), you control it directly:

  • Export: You can export your dose history as CSV from inside the app.
  • Delete: Deleting the Vialed app from your device removes the local data. To remove iCloud-synced copies, disable iCloud for Vialed in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud or delete the data from your iCloud account.

Because we do not store your data on our servers, we cannot fulfill data access or deletion requests for app data on your behalf — you are already in control.

Changes to This Policy

If we materially change this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date below and, where appropriate, surface the change in the app. Continued use of Vialed after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email support@vialed.app.
